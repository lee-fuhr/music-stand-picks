How we choose our picks

We do not claim to run our own stress tests or timed trials. Instead, our recommendations are built from three sources: the reputation of established brands among working musicians, long-term owner feedback collected from forums and retail reviews, and verified expert evaluations from published sources such as gear magazines and YouTube channels dedicated to instrument accessories. We pay close attention to what actually breaks. The most common failure points on music stands are the desk-to-stem joint, the height lock mechanism, and the non-slip feet. By filtering for models with historically low failure rates on those components and a strong track record of manufacturer support, we arrive at picks that should serve you well for years, not weeks.
