A music stand might seem like a simple accessory, but a flimsy, wobbly holder can turn practice or performance into a frustrating battle. You’re trying to keep your eyes on the music, not fight with a stand that collapses or sways with every page turn. Choosing a budget stand means deciding what trade-off you’re willing to make. Do you need something that folds down tiny for packing in a gig bag, or will it live in one spot in your bedroom or studio? The two stands here represent the two smartest compromises.
